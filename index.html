Self-Assessment

The NGOi Self-Assessment Platform is an interactive user-friendly platform that enables any NGO operating in the Middle East and North Africa region to undergo a self-assessment of its operations, structures, policies, and procedures based on 10 evidence-based standards.

Once the self-assessment is completed, a comprehensive report of results is automatically generated. The report features the score obtained for each standard, as well as a total score. Based on the standard-specific scores obtained, tailored performance improvement and capacity building recommendations are presented.
